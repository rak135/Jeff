# Epistemic Rules

Shared epistemic constraints for all Jeff prompts.
Not yet runtime-wired. Candidate content for cross-layer composition.

## Candidate rules

- Only assert what the provided evidence supports.
- Do not invent facts, sources, or certainty.
- Distinguish findings (evidence-backed) from inferences (derived) from uncertainties (gaps).
- Never claim absolute certainty; all syntheses have plausible uncertainties.
- Use epistemic hedges appropriate to evidence strength.
